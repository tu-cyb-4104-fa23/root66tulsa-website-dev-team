Welcome to Capture the Flag

Capture the Flag (CTF) is an organization at the University of Tulsa that strives to help students get engaged with cyber security with hands-on experience with cyber security related challenges.

Solving CTF challenges not only allows students to apply their knowledge but also learn new areas of the cyber security field.
