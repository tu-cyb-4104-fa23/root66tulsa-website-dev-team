About the Organization:

Capture the Flag (CTF) is an organization at the University of Tulsa that strives to help students get engaged with cyber security with hands-on experience with cyber security related challenges.

Solving CTF challenges not only allows students to apply their knowledge but also learn new areas of the cyber security field.

What is a CTF?

CTF is a competition where competitors form teams to solve challenges. These challenges can include but not limited to programming challenges, web exploitation, reverse engineering, cryptography, and miscellaneous.

How Can I Join?

Join the weekly CTF meetings that take place on Tuesdays 5:00 p.m.at Keplinger 2005. You will not only meet students who are dedicated to learning but also be able to learn new concepts and compete in CTF competitions!
