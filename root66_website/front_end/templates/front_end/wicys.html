TU Women in Cyber Security (TU WiCyS)

Who are We?

The TU student chapter of Women in Cyber Security (WiCyS) was established in 2019 and is part of the global WiCyS network. TU WiCyS is dedicated to the recruitment, retention, and advancement of young women entering the cyber security space. We host technical and networking events throughout the academic year and send students to the WiCyS conference every year. If you are interested in learning more about TU WiCyS or are a prospective student who would like to be involved, please reach out to the faculty advisor Codi West at codiwest@utulsa.edu or join the TU WiCyS discord below.

For more information about the global Women in Cyber Security organization, visit wicys.org.
